Most workflow leaks are not dramatic failures. They are tiny misses that repeat all week.

## Where leaks usually happen

- Lead intake notes are incomplete
- Scope details are not confirmed before dispatch
- Jobs close in the field but invoice data is missing
- Follow-up tasks sit in someone's inbox

<Stat label="Typical closeout lag in stressed teams" value="2-5 days" />

<Callout
  title="Simple standard"
  body="Every completed job should have a same-day closeout checklist with owner and timestamp."
/>

When closeout lags, invoicing lags. When invoicing lags, AR ages. That is how workflow issues become cash-flow pressure.
