If cash is tight, the problem is often not sales. It is timing. You did the work, sent the invoice, and now the money is sitting in accounts receivable.

## The first move is visibility, not pressure

Before you call anyone, segment AR into three buckets: 0-30, 31-60, and 61+ days. Most teams skip this and chase everything randomly.

<Stat label="Common hidden leak" value="14+ days invoice lag" />

## Run a weekly 30-minute recovery block

- Review the top 10 oldest invoices first.
- Assign one owner for each account follow-up.
- Log next action dates so nobody slips through.
- Escalate accounts over 60 days to owner review.
