If you are considering an audit, you should know what the process is and what it is not.

It is not a giant consulting engagement. It is a focused operator review designed to identify the highest-value fixes first.

## The process in plain English

- Discovery call to understand pressure points
- Review of financial and workflow signals
- Prioritized 90-day action plan with owners and timelines
