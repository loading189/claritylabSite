Most owners look at total weekly revenue and miss what is happening at the technician level. Utilization tells you whether labor is actually translating into billable work.

## Track two numbers every week

- Billable hours per technician
- Revenue per technician day

If either trend drops for three weeks, investigate dispatch gaps, callback rates, and parts delays before you blame pricing.

  body="One idle hour per tech per day compounds into serious monthly margin erosion."

Better utilization is not about squeezing people. It is about reducing dead space in the workflow so good technicians can stay in productive motion.
